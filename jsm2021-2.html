JSM 2021 SSDSE Sessions

Last updated: 2021-08-30

The ASA Section on Statistics and Data Science Education (SSDSE) is sponsoring several invited, contributed, and topic-contributed sessions at the 2021 Joint Statistical Meetings.

To make things easier for section members, check out the SSDSE-sponsored sessions below! Participation is open to JSM attendees only.

  • The full conference program is also available online.

  • Thanks to 2021 SSDSE Program Chair Jamie Perrett for compiling this list!
